Membership Benefits

NP Membership is for non profit organizations and there is no fee. This membership includes access to listings on the home page spotlights, event discounts, and networking opportunites.

Bronze Membership costs $25 a year. This membership includes all NP membership benefits as well as additional advertising opportunites, priotority even registrations, and priority support.

Silver Membership costs $50 a year. This membership includes all Bronze membership benefits as well as enhanced advertising options, increased event discounts, and premium support.

Gold Membership costs $75 a year. This membership includes all Silver membership benefits as well as exclusive advertising benefits, highest event discounts, and priority access to resources and support.
