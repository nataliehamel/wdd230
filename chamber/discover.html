Demographic and Statistical Information

Shaver has a large outdoor community. Ranging from skiing in the winter time up at China Peak, to boating at Shaver Lake in the summer time. Our community also takes advantage of the beautiful lanscape and enjoys camping, hiking, fishing, and much more.
